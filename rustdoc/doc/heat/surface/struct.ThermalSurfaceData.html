Expand description

This is a Surface from the point of view of our thermal solver. Since this module only calculate heat transfer (and not short-wave solar radiation, e.g., light), both simple_model::Fenestration and simple_model::Surface are treated in the same way.

The thermal absorbtance on the back side (from 0 to 1)

area: Float

The area of the Surface

perimeter: Float

The perimeter of the surface

normal: Vector3D

The normal of the surface

wind_speed_modifier: Float

The wind velocity changes with altitude. This field stores the factor with which the wind velocity of the weather file needs to be multiplied in order to estimate the wind speed at the exterior of the surface.

cos_tilt: Float

The cosine of the tilt angle (normal * Vector3D(0., 0., 1.))

massive_chunks: Vec<(usize, usize)>

The chunks of nodes that have mass

nomass_chunks: Vec<(usize, usize)>

The chunks of nodes that have nomass

front_alphas: Matrix

The absorbtances of each node in the system, proportional to the front incident radiation (i.e., they do not add up to 1.0)

back_alphas: Matrix

The absorbtances of each node in the system, proportional to the back incident radiation (i.e., they do not add up to 1.0)

front_hs: Option<Float>

[Only available during testing] this allows setting a fixed convection coefficient
